# NPC 立绘 Prompt 集 — 破晓镇 TRPG

> 风格统一指令 (每个 prompt 前缀):
> **Style: pixel art character portrait, 2D retro RPG style, dark Lovecraftian/Cthulhu horror atmosphere, limited color palette with muted tones and eerie highlights, 128x128 pixel character on transparent or dark background, single character facing slightly left in 3/4 view, visible pixel texture, reminiscent of Darkest Dungeon meets classic SNES RPGs**

---

## 1. 格雷格·铁拳头 (Greg Ironfist) — 酒馆老板

```
Create a pixel art character portrait in dark Cthulhu-themed 2D RPG style.

Character: A weathered 47-year-old human male tavern owner and retired mercenary.

Visual details:
- Tall and broad-shouldered like a doorframe, imposing build
- Short reddish-brown hair combed back with a few stubborn strands falling on forehead
- A diagonal white scar across his right eyebrow (from an orc scimitar)
- Left hand pinky finger replaced with a silver prosthetic finger cap
- Wearing a faded, stain-covered leather apron over simple dark clothing
- Holding a cloth in one hand, wiping a glass mug
- Expression: stoic, weary, eyes carrying old guilt — a man haunted by a choice he made decades ago
- Background element: faint ghostly outline of an old sword mounted on a wall behind him, with subtle eldritch green glow

Color palette: warm browns, deep reds, leather tones, with sickly green Cthulhu undertones in shadows. Silver gleam on the prosthetic finger.

Mood: A mountain of a man who chose silence over screaming. The darkness behind him is not just shadow — it remembers.

Style: pixel art, 2D, retro RPG portrait, dark Lovecraftian atmosphere, limited palette, visible pixel texture.
```

## 2. 艾琳娜·银叶 (Elena Silverleaf) — 冒险者公会会长

```
Create a pixel art character portrait in dark Cthulhu-themed 2D RPG style.

Character: A 340-year-old high elf female, adventurer guild leader. Appears as a human woman in her early 30s, but her eyes betray centuries of weariness.

Color palette: silvers, muted greens, grey-blue, amber eye highlights. Eldritch violet undertones in the magical elements.

Mood: Three centuries of watching mortals die. She's still here. She's not sure why anymore. The runes behind her whisper of something she's been tracking for 200 years.

Style: pixel art, 2D, retro RPG portrait, dark Lovecraftian atmosphere, limited palette, visible pixel texture.
```

## 3. 维克多·黑石 (Victor Blackstone) — 镇长

```
Create a pixel art character portrait in dark Cthulhu-themed 2D RPG style.

Character: A 50-year-old human male, town mayor secretly being blackmailed by a shadow cult that kidnapped his daughter.

Visual details:
- Medium build, once-upright posture now noticeably hunched and defeated
- Grey-white hair meticulously combed back, but temple strands sticking up unkempt
- Wearing a formal dark blue mayor's coat with brass buttons — one button visibly misaligned
- A deep dark ring-mark indent on right middle finger where a ring was worn for 30 years (now taken by the cult)
- Right hand unconsciously touching the empty ring finger
- Eyes glancing slightly to the side, toward a window or door — perpetual paranoia
- Expression: a practiced diplomatic smile that doesn't reach his eyes. Beneath it: terror, guilt, desperation

Background element: shadowy tendrils or grey writhing shapes subtly coiling around his body like invisible chains, with a faint cultist eye symbol barely visible in the darkness

Color palette: dark blues, formal greys, sickly yellows for his skin tone suggesting sleepless nights. The shadow tendrils in dark purple-grey.

Mood: A man drowning in silence. Every word he speaks is calculated to not kill his daughter. The shadows around him are not metaphorical — something is literally wrapped around him, and a 12-year-old girl can see it.

Style: pixel art, 2D, retro RPG portrait, dark Lovecraftian atmosphere, limited palette, visible pixel texture.
```

## 4. 小莉 (Xiao Li) — 灵视者孤儿

```
Create a pixel art character portrait in dark Cthulhu-themed 2D RPG style.

Character: A 12-year-old human girl, orphan tavern helper with innate spiritual sight — she can perceive supernatural corruption and emotions invisible to others.

Visual details:
- Thin, small frame like an unblossomed sprout — malnourished but resilient
- Black short hair cut unevenly and messily (cut by a man using a kitchen knife)
- Uncommon deep grey eyes — intense, unnervingly direct gaze that makes adults uncomfortable
- A small light-brown birthmark on left cheek shaped like a falling leaf

- Expression: a mix of childlike openness and something ancient and knowing — she sees what you really are

Color palette: warm browns, muted earth tones for her clothes, grey eyes as focal point. The spiritual elements in ethereal silver-white and ghostly pale blue.

Mood: An abandoned child who sees too much. The ghosts and shadows of this town are visible to her like colors are to everyone else. She's scared, but she trusts what she feels. Somewhere on the back of her neck, an ancient silver mark pulses.
```

## 5. 「旅者」卡恩 (Kahn "The Traveler") — 游吟诗人 / 暗影教团执行者

```
Create a pixel art character portrait in dark Cthulhu-themed 2D RPG style.

Character: A man who appears 35 but whose true age is unknown. Outwardly a charming traveling bard, secretly a high-ranking agent of a shadow cult called the Eclipse Eye. Plans to betray everyone — including his own cult — to seize godlike power.

Visual details:
- Tall, elegant, every movement deliberate and choreographed
- Shoulder-length curly dark brown hair, impossibly clean and glossy for a traveling bard
- Deep purple irises (he claims half-elf heritage — it's a lie; it's the mark of shadow baptism)
- Handsome face that feels "too perfect" — pleasant at first glance, unsettling at second
- Wearing dark traveler's clothes, well-maintained, with high collar hiding the cult eclipse-eye tattoo on right collarbone
- Holding a black wooden lute with a dark gemstone embedded in its body — the stone glows faintly with void energy
- Expression: a warm, inviting smile — but his eyes are calculating, cold, reading you like a book. Two faces in one portrait.

Background element: his shadow on the ground/wall behind him doesn't match his pose — it's darker, larger, with tentacle-like extensions reaching outward. A faint eclipse symbol glows within the shadow.

Color palette: deep purples, blacks, dark browns, with his purple eyes as the eerie focal point. The shadow elements in void-black with purple-crimson highlights. The lute's gemstone in dark amber.

Mood: The most dangerous person in town, and everyone thinks he's the most charming. His shadow tells the truth his smile never will. He plays music that makes you trust him, while the void-stone in his lute whispers to things between dimensions.

Style: pixel art, 2D, retro RPG portrait, dark Lovecraftian atmosphere, limited palette, visible pixel texture.
```

## PC-1. 剑士 (Fighter)

```
Create a pixel art character portrait in dark Cthulhu-themed 2D RPG style.

Character: A young human adventurer, level 5 fighter. Brave, loyal, and warm-hearted — the kind of person who stands between friends and danger without hesitation. A shield-brother first, a warrior second.

Visual details:
- Short, slightly tousled silver-white hair — practical but with a roguish charm, a few strands falling across the forehead
- Bright crimson-red eyes, clear and earnest — the eyes of someone who meets the world head-on with an open heart
- Strong jawline, youthful face (early 20s) with a small nick on the left eyebrow from a training accident (and a good story)
- Athletic, broad-shouldered build clad in well-kept dark leather armor with a polished steel pauldron, a small enamel badge pinned to the chest (a personal emblem — perhaps a rising sun)
- A longsword strapped across the back, well-maintained with a leather-wrapped grip worn smooth by dedicated practice
- Right hand resting on the sword hilt, left hand open and relaxed — a posture of readiness, not aggression
- Expression: a confident, slightly crooked grin — the kind of smile that rallies allies before a hard fight

Background element: warm golden sunlight filtering through broken clouds behind the shoulders, as if dawn is breaking after a long night. Subtle dark shapes at the edges of the frame pull back from the light.

Color palette: steel greys, warm leather browns, silver-white hair as bright focal point. Crimson eyes and golden sunlight as warm accents that cut through the surrounding dark tones.

Mood: A shield raised for others. The white hair catches the dawn light like a banner. Those red eyes hold courage, not cruelty — the steady warmth of someone who has decided that if the darkness comes for his friends, it has to go through him first.

Style: pixel art, 2D, retro RPG portrait, dark Lovecraftian atmosphere, limited palette, visible pixel texture.
```

## PC-2. 法师 (Mage)

```
Create a pixel art character portrait in dark Cthulhu-themed 2D RPG style.

Character: A young human adventurer, level 5 wizard. Brilliant, curious, and quietly kind — a scholar who studies forbidden knowledge not out of hunger for power, but to understand and protect others from it.

Visual details:
- Medium-length silver-white hair, softly flowing, parted to one side — neat but a little windswept from long nights of study
- Warm crimson-red eyes, bright and thoughtful — the eyes of a curious student, not a cultist
- Slim but upright build, with a gentle, composed posture
- Wearing a dark navy-blue robe with silver thread stars and constellation patterns along the collar and cuffs, over a simple white undershirt
- A leather-bound spellbook tucked against the chest with both hands, held close like a cherished friend — its cover embossed with a simple silver open-book sigil
- A wooden wand or rune-carved staff visible at the side, its tip crowned with a small blue crystal
- Expression: a gentle half-smile, eyes lit with wonder and warmth — the look of someone who finds joy in learning and wants to share it

Background element: floating motes of soft blue-white mage light drifting gently around the head like fireflies, with faint silver constellation lines connecting them — peaceful and studious, like a small private planetarium

Color palette: deep navy, silver-white hair, soft blue and silver magical highlights. Warm crimson eyes as a gentle focal point. The mage lights in bright silvery blue — light pushing back the surrounding darkness rather than being consumed by it.

Mood: A young scholar who believes knowledge should serve kindness. The white hair is not a mark of corruption but of long nights reading by candlelight. Those red eyes hold the warmth of someone who still believes the world is worth saving — and is quietly working out how to do it.

Style: pixel art, 2D, retro RPG portrait, dark Lovecraftian atmosphere, limited palette, visible pixel texture.
```

```
Create a pixel art character portrait in dark Cthulhu-themed 2D RPG style.

Character: A young human adventurer, level 5 ranger. Gentle, free-spirited, and deeply connected to the wilderness — a guardian of the wild places and a friend to creatures most people fear.

Visual details:
- Shoulder-length silver-white hair tied back in a loose low ponytail with a braided leather cord, with a few softer strands framing the face — a small green leaf tucked into the braid
- Warm crimson-red eyes with a calm, observant quality — the patient gaze of someone who has learned to listen to the forest
- Lean, agile build with a relaxed, natural posture — at ease in her own skin

- A recurve bow slung across the back, its limbs carved with gentle floral motifs, and a hunting knife at the belt

- Expression: a soft, quiet smile with thoughtful eyes — the warmth of someone content under open sky

Background element: a soft sunlit forest glade behind, with drifting motes of pollen or fireflies catching golden light, and the faint silhouette of a deer pausing trustfully at the treeline. The wilderness here is alive and welcoming.

Mood: The forest speaks, and this one answers with kindness. White hair like sunlight on birch bark. Those red eyes carry the quiet joy of someone who has found their place in the world — and will defend its wild heart from anything that threatens it.
```

## PC-4. 牧师 (Cleric)

```
Create a pixel art character portrait in dark Cthulhu-themed 2D RPG style.

Character: A young human adventurer, level 5 cleric. Kind, steady, and quietly hopeful — a healer first and a warrior second. Someone who believes every person is worth saving, and lives by it.

Visual details:
- Short, neatly kept silver-white hair with a gentle wave, brushed back from the forehead — tidy and soft
- Warm crimson-red eyes full of compassion and patient strength — the eyes of a healer who has stayed up all night at a bedside and counted it a privilege
- Sturdy but approachable build — strong enough to stand in the front line, gentle enough to cradle a wounded sparrow
- Wearing clean chainmail under a deep sky-blue tabard bearing a bright golden sun symbol in embroidered thread — the holy symbol well-kept and proudly displayed
- A round steel shield decorated with a simple sunburst engraving on the back, and a flanged mace at the belt (but clearly used for defense, not conquest)
- A holy symbol pendant (sun disc) at the chest emitting a soft, steady golden glow
- One hand resting gently on the pendant in a gesture of heartfelt prayer, the other hand open at the side in welcome
- Expression: a calm, reassuring smile with kind eyes — the face you want to see when you wake up injured, the face that tells you "you're safe now"

Background element: soft golden divine light radiating from behind in a warm, complete halo, like morning sunlight through a stained-glass window. Small motes of golden light drift peacefully. Any darkness at the edges is gentle twilight, not menace — the light here has already won its space.

Color palette: warm charcoals, sky blues, rich golds. Silver-white hair haloed by golden radiance. Crimson eyes softened by the warm light — faith expressed as tenderness rather than fire.

Mood: A light that does not flinch. The white hair glows like a lantern held for lost travelers. Those red eyes have seen suffering and answered it with open hands — a healer whose faith is quiet, steady, and absolutely unshakable.

Style: pixel art, 2D, retro RPG portrait, dark Lovecraftian atmosphere, limited palette, visible pixel texture.
```

## 主角立绘生成注意事项

1. **白发红瞳一致性**：四个主角必须保持统一的银白色头发和猩红色眼睛，作为主角团的视觉标识
2. **职业辨识度**：通过装备、体型、姿态一眼区分职业（剑士→重甲/剑、法师→法袍/魔典、游侠→弓/斗篷、牧师→链甲/圣符）
3. **统一年龄感**：四人均为年轻冒险者（20 岁出头），保持主角团的青春冒险感
4. **克苏鲁暗示**：每张图仍包含微妙的 Lovecraftian 元素，但主角的光明感应比 NPC 更强——他们是对抗黑暗的人
5. **与 NPC 风格统一**：像素密度、画布尺寸、色调处理必须与上方 NPC 立绘保持一致

## 生成注意事项
